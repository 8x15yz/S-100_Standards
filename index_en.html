S-100 Standards Documentation

Welcome to the S-100 Standards documentation portal — a reference hub for IHO Universal Hydrographic Data Model–based product specifications.

The S-100 framework, published by the International Hydrographic Organization (IHO), is the universal hydrographic data model that underpins a new generation of digital maritime standards. This portal documents a curated set of S-100–family product specifications used in modern Electronic Navigational Chart (ENC) systems, port management, and maritime safety services.

Each standard below defines feature catalogues, portrayal catalogues, and GML schemas that enable interoperable exchange of geospatial maritime data between ships, port authorities, and maritime safety agencies.

S-102

Bathymetric Surface

S-102 specifies the content model and encoding for bathymetric surface data, providing high-resolution depth information to complement ENC data. It supports grid-based depth models used by voyage planning systems and autonomous vessel navigation.

S-124

Navigational Warnings

S-124 defines the data model for encoding and disseminating navigational warnings, replacing legacy NAVTEX and radio-based warning systems with structured, georeferenced digital messages. It enables vessels to receive and display warnings directly on ECDIS.

Detailed content will be added soon.

S-201

Aids to Navigation (AtoN)

S-201 provides a standardised data model for Aids to Navigation information — buoys, beacons, lights, and fog signals — enabling seamless exchange between AtoN authorities, vessel traffic services, and onboard navigation systems.

Detailed content will be added soon.

S-158

Ports and Waterways

S-158 covers the product specification for ports and inland waterways data, including berth geometry, fairway limits, and operational constraints. It bridges hydrographic charting and port management workflows.

Detailed content will be added soon.

S-158(100)

Ports and Waterways — S-100 Edition

S-158(100) is the S-100–compliant edition of the ports and waterways product specification, conforming to the IHO S-100 data model and leveraging GML-based feature catalogues for full interoperability with modern ECDIS platforms.

Detailed content will be added soon.

S-158·98

Ports and Waterways — S-98 Edition

S-158·98 is the S-98–based variant of the ports and waterways specification, targeting systems that implement the earlier IHO encoding framework. It provides a migration path and compatibility layer for legacy port management installations.
